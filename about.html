Choosing the Right Wetsuit

Due to the variability of water temperatures and wind conditions, The Coeur d'Alene Triathlon reccomends that all swimmers wear a wetsuit.
Athletes participating in our traditional Olympic distance (1.5K swim) will board a cruise ship and enter the water out in the bay and swim back to shore.
The “Sprint Distance Triathlon” swimmers will take off from city beach one hour after the Olympic race begins. The Scenic Sprint consists of a 500-meter swim.

There are several key factors to consider when buying your first open water wetsuit:

  • Competitiveness

  • Budget

  • Frequency of Use

  • Swim Distance

  • Water Temperature

  • Swimming Background/Skill Level

  • When to Choose a Sleeveless Suit
